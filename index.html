KULERMAPP

Kulermapp is an interactive map produced by MOOD. Using cached Twitter data over May 16, 2015, Kulermapp enables color analysis through its responsive data visualization. By studying how people in different parts of the world talk about color, MOOD hopes to provide insight into different cultures and communities.
